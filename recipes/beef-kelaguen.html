Beef Kelaguen

Home

Description

Ceviche but for Chamorros

Ingredients

  • 1 Â½ cups lemon juice, or as needed
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Tabasco to taste
  • 2 pounds beef flank steak, very thinly sliced against the grain
  • 1 bunch green onions, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Pour lemon juice, soy sauce, and Tabasco into a large, glass bowl. Stir in beef and green onions. Add additional lemon juice if needed to cover the beef.
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, and allow to rest at room temperature for 1 hour, until meat turns a grayish-brown color and appears cooked.
