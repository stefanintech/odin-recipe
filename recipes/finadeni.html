Description

Traditional sauce from Guam

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ¼ cup chopped green onions
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Combine soy sauce, lemon juice, green onions, and pepper flakes in a small bowl. Let stand before serving to allow flavors to blend, about 15 minutes.
